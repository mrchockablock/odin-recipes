Chicken Enchiladas

Description

Enchiladas are a traditional Mexican dish made of rolled corn tortillas filled with meat, cheese, beans, or vegetables, and covered with a chili pepper sauce. The dish is typically baked in the oven and served with rice and beans.

Ingredients

  • Corn tortillas
  • Shredded chicken
  • Chili pepper sauce
  • Onions
  • Garlic
  • Tomato sauce
  • Green chilies
  • Black beans
  • Corn
  • Cilantro
  • Grated cheese

Instructions

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C (350°F).
  2. Heat some oil in a large pan and fry the onions and garlic until soft.
  3. Add the shredded chicken, chili pepper sauce, tomato sauce, green chilies, black beans, corn, and cilantro. Simmer for 30 minutes.
  4. Spread a layer of the chicken mixture in a baking dish, followed by a layer of corn tortillas, and then a layer of grated cheese. Repeat until all ingredients are used up.
  5. Top with more chili pepper sauce and grated cheese and bake for 30 minutes.
